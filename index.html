Course Overview

Welcome to the Gemini Faculty Fundamentals hub. This 13-week learning path is designed to transition instructional faculty from AI curiosity to classroom mastery.

Each module includes a focused video training provided by Google, a discussion prompt for reflection, and a practical activity to implement in your own curriculum.
